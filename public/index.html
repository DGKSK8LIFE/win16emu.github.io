A simple solution, to an expensive problem

Imagine this: A simple emulator, that runs off of 2 simple dependencies, and can emulate 16bit programs quickly and seemlessly.
This is the reality of win16emu. A quick, simple, easy, highly customizable 16 bit emulator for Windows Vista-latest.
